About Turtleman

Turtleman is a free comic inspired by the great cheap superhero comics of the 60s and 70s (like the Phantom). Turtleman is an everyday man and his family that like travelling and seeing new places. Developed as a travelling aid, Turtleman leans into "AI Slop"- making it a feature, not a bug.
