About Turtleman

Turtleman is a free comic inspired by the great cheap superhero comics of the 60s and 70s (especially the Phantom). Turtleman is pointless and possibly the most useless superhero ever. Developed as a travelling aid, Turtleman leans into "AI Slop" making it a feature, not a bug.
